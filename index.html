1. About Gomoku

Gomoku (Five in a Row) is a well known board game. The board has 15 rows and 15 columns. One player has crosses, the other has circles. Players alternate in placing crosses or circles on the empty tiles on the board. The winner is the first player to get a row of five shapes horizontally, vertically or diagonally.

2. Before you start the game

If you want to play against phone, press Player vs Bot. If you want to play against another player, select Player vs Player.

3. Control of the game

In the top bar, there are shown labels of players and their shapes. The starting player changes each game. The player who is just playing is highlighted on the bar with yellow color. To put a stone (cross, circle), you have to click anywhere on the board. The board zooms at the place where you clicked and the field is marked by yellow color. By the second click you put the stone and the board automatically unzooms. In the bottom of the screen you can find buttons controlling the game. There are buttons for zoom and unzoom in the left. By clicking New Game button, you give up current match and new one is automatically started. Last button serves for returning to main menu.
